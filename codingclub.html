Learn, Build, Collaborate

Computer Science is one of the fastest growing fields in the world. There are more CompSci-related jobs available than actual people qualified to fill those jobs. Computer Science allows people to see innovations and advances in science, engineering, business, entertainment, and education. Programmers solve deep, multidimensional problems in the world that require perspective and inventiveness. The best solutions in computing are derived from creativity and imagination.

The number of open jobs in this field is constantly increasing and showing no signs of slowing down. We need the number of qualified people to increase at that same rate to meet those jobs. This is why we started Coding Club. Our club welcomes students of any skill level, from those who've never touched an editor in their life to veteran coders. Coding Club is a positive and friendly environment to work on projects, share ideas, and collaborate.
